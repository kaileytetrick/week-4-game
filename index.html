Feed the Monkey!

How to Play:

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

  2. There are four types of bananas:

    • Blue Banana
    • Green Banana
    • Pink Banana
    • Yellow Banana

  3. By clicking on one of the bananas, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

  4. If you match your total score to the random number, you win and the monkey gets fed!

  5. If your total score goes above the random number, you lose and the monkey doesn't get any bananas.

  6. The value of each banana is hidden from you until you click on it.

  7. Each time the game starts, the value of the each banana will change.
